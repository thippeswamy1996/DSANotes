In the **REMC / SAMAST (Scheduling, Accounting, Metering & Settlement Tool)** used in power & renewable energy operations, these terms have **specific operational meanings**. Below is a **clear, structured explanation** 👇

---

## 1️⃣ Declaration

**What it means:**

* A **Declaration** is the **generation availability submitted by the generator** (RE plant, wind, solar, hydro, etc.).
* It tells the system **how much power the generator can produce** for a given **time block / day**.

**Key points:**

* Submitted **before scheduling**
* Based on:

  * Weather forecast (wind/solar)
  * Machine availability
* Used by SLDC / RLDC for planning

**Example:**

> Solar plant declares **50 MW available** from 10:00–10:15

---

## 2️⃣ Entitlement

**What it means:**

* **Entitlement** is the **approved share of power allocated to a buyer / DISCOM** from the declared generation.
* Calculated based on:

  * PPAs
  * Capacity share
  * SLDC/RLDC allocation rules

**Key points:**

* Derived **after declaration**
* Shows **who is entitled to how much power**
* Used for billing & settlement

**Example:**

> From 50 MW declared:

* DISCOM A → 30 MW
* DISCOM B → 20 MW

---

## 3️⃣ Requisition

**What it means:**

* A **Requisition** is the **power demand requested by the buyer (DISCOM)** against its entitlement.
* Buyer may:

  * Requisition **full entitlement**
  * Requisition **partial power**
  * Requisition **zero power**

**Key points:**

* Raised by **DISCOM**
* Must be **≤ Entitlement**
* Helps avoid over-drawal

**Example:**

> DISCOM A entitled to 30 MW but requisitions only **25 MW**

---

## 4️⃣ Schedule

**What it means:**

* **Schedule** is the **final approved power plan** for injection (generator) and drawal (DISCOM).
* Issued by **SLDC / RLDC** after considering:

  * Declaration
  * Entitlement
  * Requisition
  * Grid constraints

**Key points:**

* **Legally binding**
* Used for:

  * Real-time operations
  * DSM / deviation settlement
* Generated for **15-min / 5-min time blocks**

**Example:**

> Final schedule approved:

* Generator → Inject 45 MW
* DISCOM A → Draw 25 MW
* DISCOM B → Draw 20 MW

---

## 🔁 End-to-End Flow (Very Important)

```
Declaration (Generator)
        ↓
Entitlement (System / SLDC)
        ↓
Requisition (DISCOM)
        ↓
Schedule (SLDC / RLDC Final Approval)
```

---

## 🧠 Simple One-Line Summary

* **Declaration** → “I can generate this much”
* **Entitlement** → “You are allowed this much”
* **Requisition** → “I want this much”
* **Schedule** → “This is the final approved plan”
